About Brittany

Brittany has been interested in design since childhood. Growing up you would always catch her sketching cartoons, creating graphic novels, designing and sewing clothes and accessories, and creating her own art projects out of anything she could find. Brittany always found a way to put a creative spin on everything. Brittany also loved to knit scarves, blankets, belts, and runners - she pretty much made anything square.

As she got older, technology grew and so did her passion to create. She used technology to do anything from creating random shapes in Paint application and coloring them to using sites to create her own personal websites to creating fliers for local businesses all before high school.

She has always had a passion for design and technology. After extensive research, Brittany discovered the world of UX Design. After researching, she took a leap of faith and applied to Northwestern’s UX/UI Bootcamp and hasn’t looked back!
